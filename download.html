30 Practice Quotes To Keep You Motivated And Training Hard

Written by
Paul Smith
Quotes /
Last Updated: March 17, 2023 1:18 pm

Whilst it might seem that some are simply born talented, you can be sure that the pros never achieved their success without practice.

Therefore, whether you’re starting a new hobby, job, or business, it’s essential to practice regularly. Practicing your skills ensures that you will progress, become more competent, and achieve success.

In fact, practicing is the greatest way to reach the top in any field, whether it be sports, music, or business.

To help you on your way, here are the top 30 quotes about practice to help keep you motivated and training hard when the going gets tough!

This collection of quotes will inspire you on your path to absolute mastery, serving as a great reminder that hard work always pays off.

8 Practice Quotes To Help Improve Your Skills

If you want to improve your skills or knowledge, then practice is the key.

Here are some quotes that will help you on your own journey of improvement. Such quotes are not only an inspiration to do something good but can also help to boost your confidence.

#1

If you don’t practice you don’t deserve to win. Andre Agassi

#2

I know you’ve heard it a thousand times before. But it’s true – hard work pays off. If you want to be good, you have to practice, practice, practice. If you don’t love something, then don’t do it. Ray Bradbury

#3

The more you practice, the better you get, the more freedom you have to create. Jocko Willink

#4

Courage is the most important of all virtues because without courage you can’t practice any other virtue consistently. You can practice any virtue erratically, but nothing consistently without courage. Maya Angelou

#5

Knowledge is of no value unless you put it into practice. Anton Chekhov 

#6

If I don’t practice one day, I know it; two days, the critics know it; three days, the public knows it. Jascha Heifetz

#7

The more you practice and study, the better you are so I still practice and study all the time. Cyndi Lauper

#8

What can cake teach you about life? That practice makes perfect, and if you try something once, it probably wont be perfect, and you have to keep working on it if you want to be good at it. Nicole Byer

7 Practice Quotes To Motivate You On Your Journey of Improvement

When you are traveling along the path of improvement, you might face different obstacles, but if you maintain your confidence, everything will work out in the end.

So, if you want to keep practicing and getting better at what you do, here is a collection of practice quotes to motivate you on your journey.

#1

Practice puts brains in your muscles. Sam Snead

#2

Practice is the best of all instructors.   Publilius Syrus

#3

Take chances, make mistakes. That’s how you grow. Pain nourishes your courage. You have to fail in order to practice being brave. Mary Tyler Moore

#4

Practice every time you get a chance. Bill Monroe

#5

Everything is practice. Pele

#6

If you want others to be happy, practice compassion. If you want to be happy, practice compassion. Dalai Lama

#7

An ounce of practice is worth more than tons of preaching. Mahatma Gandhi

7 Practice Quotes To Help You Get To The Top

Motivation and practice go hand in hand. They are both necessary if your goal is to reach the top.

Yet, without a clear and focused plan, it might take you a long time to get there. However, with both motivation and practice, it can happen quicker than you think. 

These motivational practice quotes will inspire you to stay focused and in the zone so you can fulfill all your potential and reap the benefits.

#1

I always say that practice gets you to the top most of the time. David Beckham

#2

If I dont practice the way I should, then I wont play the way that I know I can. Ivan Lendl

#3

The more I practice the luckier I get. Gary Player

#4

Sincere practice makes the impossible possible.   Dada Vaswani

#5

You’ve got to learn your instrument. Then, you practice, practice, practice. And then, when you finally get up there on the bandstand, forget all that and just wail. Charlie Parker

#6

Its not necessarily the amount of time you spend at practice that counts; its what you put into the practice.   Eric Lindros

#7

Practice does not make perfect. Only perfect practice makes perfect. Vince Lombardi

8 Practice Quotes To Inspire Growth And Progress

This list of practice quotes can help teach you the importance of practice. If you are an athlete who is training, or just someone who is pursuing their passion, these inspirational practice quotes are sure to get your mind ticking. 

These quotes will help push you further, and maybe even teach you something new about yourself.

#1

Sometimes the best, and only effective, way to kill an idea is to put it into practice. Sydney J. Harris

#2

I put my ideas into practice. That may be the reason people hate me. Emir Kusturica 

#3

Take advantage of every opportunity to practice your communication skills so that when important occasions arise, you will have the gift, the style, the sharpness, the clarity, and the emotions to affect other people.   Jim Rohn

#4

Failure happens all the time. It happens every day in practice. What makes you better is how you react to it. Mia Hamm

#5

Practice makes permanent. Bobby Robson

#6

We cannot make good news out of bad practice. Edward R. Murrow

#7

Practice means to perform, over and over again in the face of all obstacles, some act of vision, of faith, of desire. Practice is a means of inviting the perfection desired. Martha Graham

#8

You can’t hire someone to practice for you. H. Jackson Brown, Jr.

That’s All!

We hope that some of these quotes will help you to keep motivated in your journey or training. Whether you’re a fitness professional or an athlete, these quotes are great reminders of the importance of practice and dedication. They serve to remind us that practice is crucial to improving our skills, knowledge, and understanding.

Share this collection of quotes about practice with anyone in your life who values personal development and growth.
